Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026

1. Introduction

hibikishimizu.com is a personal portfolio website operated by Hibiki Shimizu. This Privacy Policy explains how information collected through this site is handled.

2. Information Collected

This site may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and message content submitted via the contact form
  • Cookie data for analytics (Google Analytics)
  • Server logs (IP address, browser info, access date/time)

3. Purpose of Use

  • To respond to contact enquiries
  • To analyse and improve the site
  • To prevent spam and unauthorised access

4. Third-Party Disclosure

Personal information collected will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

5. Cookies

This site may use Google Analytics to analyse site traffic. Google Analytics collects anonymous traffic data via cookies. You can disable cookies through your browser settings. For more details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention & Security

Information submitted via the contact form is received by email and managed appropriately. Information no longer needed will be promptly deleted.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, please use the contact page or email hibiki.shimizu.jp@gmail.com.

8. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated without prior notice. Any changes will be posted on this page.
